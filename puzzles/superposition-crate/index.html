Interference is like musicians in a band: if all of them are in harmony, the music is also harmonous and is amplified. However, if they are in conflict, then the music is chaotic and its effect is reduce, or even inaudible.

Similarly, quantum interference happens when quantum objects interact with each other. When quantum interference happens, quantum states might be cancelled out or become amplified.

This allows us to remove the unwanted quantum state and amplify the wanted ones.

Rules:
  • The goal of this puzzle is to stablise all quantum crates.
  • Each quantum crate is in a superposition state of stable and unstable. They are represented by waveforms.
  • There are two things you need to accomplish: amplifying the stable state, and cancelling the unstable state.
  • To amplify the stable state, calibrate the device such that its waveform matches that of the stable state.
  • To cancel the unstable state, calibrate the device such that its waveform is the inversion of the waveform of the unstable state.
