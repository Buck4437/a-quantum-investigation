Rules:
  • There are four levels in total.
  • Each level consists a package with a fixed number of quantum balls.
  • It is given that all the quantum balls are in a superposition state of two different locations.
  • Your target is to restore the original states of the label.
  • You can open the package to gain information on the positions of the balls. After opening the package, you can reset the package back to the superposition states.
  • To label a ball, click on the two cells that a ball is in superposition state of.
