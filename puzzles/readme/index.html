Introduction

This is a puzzle hunt. Your primary objective is to solve puzzles by entering memos on the main page. Even though this puzzle hunt is based on quantum mechanics, pre-requisite knowledge is not required. They will be introduced throughout the puzzle hunt.

Most puzzles come with interactive components. Most of them are experiments involving quantum mechanics or analogies of concepts in quantum physics. However, some of them will involve puzzle-solving elements.

Solving puzzles

All answers in this puzzle hunt will be referred to as memos. If you see the word memo, or when things are highlighted in aqua, chances are, the highlighted text is / is related to the answer to the puzzle.

All answers are common English words. Furthermore, except for the final puzzle, all answers will be given in plain text and IN UPPERCASE. The answer is case-insensitive, and all space characters are removed automatically.

Note that your puzzle progresses are not saved. All progress will be lost when you reload the pages. I highly recommend keeping a backup of all puzzle answers in case the page is reloaded automatically.

Solutions and afterwords

I highly recommend checking this page ONLY AFTER you have solved the final puzzle.

All solutions and afterwords can be found on this page. If you are stuck, you can find solutions to most puzzles here.
